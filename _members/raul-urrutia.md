Dr. Raul Urrutia is an internationally recognized physician-scientist whose contributions have shaped the fields of gene regulation and pancreatic disease research. Through pioneering studies in chromatin biology and translational medicine, he has driven innovations that bridge basic science and clinical application. In addition to his scientific leadership, he is widely respected for his mentorship, fostering interdisciplinary teams and guiding trainees toward impactful careers in biomedical research.
